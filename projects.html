Major works executed by Galaxy Concepts Limited

We have done and completed several projects.

SBC (Uganda) Limited

Galaxy Concepts Limited executed major works for the client, SBC (Uganda) Limited, entailing civil works for the primary campsite in the development of Kabaale International Airport. The tasks encompassed clearing and grubbing, topsoil removal (40cm), and filling G15. This project took place from 2018 to 2019, with a contract value of USD 475,000

National Resistance Movement (NRM) Party

Galaxy Concepts executed this substantial project for the National Resistance Movement (NRM) Party, entailing the prefabrication and erection of billboards across 6200 sites in Uganda. The tasks included identifying suitable sites, erecting billboards, and effectively pitching messages. The project was successfully completed in the year 2020.

Sadeem Al Kuwait General Trading and Contracting Company

Galaxy Concepts executed this significant project for Sadeem Al Kuwait General Trading and Contracting Company, involving civil works for the upgrading of the Rwenkunye–Apac-Lira-Puranga road. The tasks included constructing prefab houses, rock filling, as well as bush clearing and ground leveling. This project was successfully completed within the period of 2021-2022

Dott Services Limited

Galaxy Concepts did this notable project for Dott Services Limited, which involved civil works for the upgrading of the Ishaka–Kagamba road. The tasks encompassed the design and fabrication of signage materials. This project was effectively carried out during the timeframe of 2019-2020.

Nexus Green Limited

Galaxy Concepts did this significant project for Nexus Green Limited, which involved civil works for the establishment and removal of site signboards for up to 360 sites in the main contract. The tasks primarily revolved around the establishment and subsequent removal of these signboards. The project was efficiently completed within the timeframe of 2021-2022.
